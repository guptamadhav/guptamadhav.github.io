HandleIt

AI-Powered Adaptive Fine Motor Rehabilitation Platform

Computer Vision Generative AI RAG Healthcare PyTorch

Overview

HandleIt is an AI-powered rehabilitation platform designed to help patients with fine motor skill challenges. The platform combines computer vision, generative AI, and RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) models to create personalized rehabilitation experiences. The system adapts to each patient's progress and provides real-time feedback through gamified modules and voice commands for enhanced accessibility.

Key Achievements

  • ▸ Led development of scalable Computer Vision and AI pipelines integrating Generative AI and RAG models
  • ▸ Architected end-to-end user journey and treatment workflow engine for personalized rehabilitation paths
  • ▸ Engineered multimodal accessibility features including voice commands and gamified therapy modules
  • ▸ Finalist in Primary Care Pitch Competition, demonstrating clinical viability
  • ▸ Secured $5,000 in grants with validated market fit through pilot testing

Technical Implementation

Computer Vision Pipeline: Developed real-time hand tracking and gesture recognition using PyTorch-based models to monitor patient movements and provide instant feedback on exercise form and completion.

RAG Integration: Implemented a Retrieval-Augmented Generation system to provide personalized treatment recommendations based on medical literature and patient history, enabling adaptive therapy plans.

Generative AI: Utilized generative models to create customized exercise variations and difficulty levels that adapt to each patient's progress and capabilities.

Accessibility Features: Built voice command interface and gamification layer to improve engagement and accessibility for patients with varying abilities.

Impact

HandleIt demonstrates the potential of AI-driven healthcare solutions to improve patient outcomes in rehabilitation. The platform's success in competitions and grant acquisition validates its clinical approach and market potential. By combining multiple AI technologies, the system provides a comprehensive, accessible rehabilitation solution that adapts to individual patient needs.

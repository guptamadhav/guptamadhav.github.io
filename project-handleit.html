HandleIt

AI-Powered Fine Motor Rehabilitation Platform

Innovation and Clinical Integrations Lead Computer Vision RAG Healthcare Hackathon

Overview

HandleIt helps older adults practice fine motor skills through AI-powered adaptive therapy. Built during a 40-hour hackathon, we validated it with real user feedback and later expanded it through SPARK and CABHI programs. The project reached the Top 8 of Edmonton Startup Week’s Primary Care Pitch Competition.

My Contributions

  • ▸ Built the backend for real-time hand tracking and gesture recognition using YOLO and Mediapipe.
  • ▸ Designed the user journey and adaptive treatment workflows for fine motor exercises, guided by occupational therapy expertise.
  • ▸ Implemented voice command interface and gamified modules to improve accessibility and engagement.
  • ▸ Collaborating with a team of 3 + occupational therapist to integrate AI, clinical insights, and UX into a functional rehab platform.
  • ▸ Validated the prototype within 40 hours during a hackathon through real user testing and secured $5,000+ in grants.

Featured project

Wallet

Personal credit card co-pilot for Canadians. You add your cards; Wallet tells you which one to use and why—by merchant and category—so you always get the best rewards from the cards you already have.

Runs fully on your device. It ingests reward data from six major Canadian banks (RBC, TD, CIBC, Scotiabank, BMO, ATB): 100+ cards, 900+ multiplier entries, 40+ merchants, 30+ MCC codes, 36+ partner programs. A small language model runs locally and is fine-tuned on 1,000+ queries to explain recommendations in short, clear language. PostgreSQL holds static card and reward data; a vector DB pulls in new offers. You can tilt recommendations (e.g. airline miles vs cashback). In progress: a 7-day cash-flow forecast using your transaction history and synthetic data for R&D. Started in the Edmonton Unlimited Student Founder's program; preparing for production.
